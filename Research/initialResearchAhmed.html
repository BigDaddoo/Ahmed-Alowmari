Initial Research

Problem Statement

After a global pandemic and quarantine, students have experienced extreme struggles such as rapidly declining mental health, higher rates of violence and misbehavior, post-traumatic stress symptoms, financial and food insecurities, lack of direction or guidance, world-wide disorder, and much more. One of the biggest effects was the decline of social-emotional ability which includes self-awareness, self-management, responsible decision-making, social awareness, and relationship skills.

Need Statement

Students need some sort of service that can help them regain and develop their social-emotional skills so they can identify their feelings, understand and communicate with others, build strong relationships, and make good, empathetic decisions.
