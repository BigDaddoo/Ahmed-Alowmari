Water is unevenly distributed around the world. Large portions of the world receive very little water relative to their population and needs.

There are many ways we can combat water inequality.

Here are 8 ways we can do so:

  1. Education/Awareness

    • Advertisement and posters on water
  2. New Conservation Technologies
  3. Recycle Wastewater

  7. Rain Water Harvesting
  8. Community Governance and Partnerships
